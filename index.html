_KNOWLEDGE

I'm a digital and analog designer. I'm a self-taught Ruby on Rails(my stack: SublimeText, Git/GitHub, Heroku, Linux terminal) and front-end (HTML/CSS) developer, working on my own startups and clients projects.

I use Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign,architectural softwares like Autocad, Revit, and Sketchup. And for the record, I’m also very good at drawing and illustrating, both analog and digital. I also know how to manipulate sounds using Sony Acid Pro and Sony Soundforge.

I've organized various meetups, local markets and collectives through the last 3 years. I organize teams, make products and hack processes.
